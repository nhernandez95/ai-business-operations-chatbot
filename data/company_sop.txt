Vendor Onboarding Policy:
All new vendors must submit a W-9, banking information, point of contact, insurance documentation, and signed service agreement. Finance reviews payment terms before approval.

Refund Policy:
Customer refunds must be reviewed within 3 business days. Refunds over $1,000 require manager approval and supporting documentation.

Weekly KPI Reporting:
Operations teams must submit weekly KPIs every Friday by 3 PM. Reports must include revenue, open issues, completed work, SLA exceptions, and risk notes.

Data Quality Process:
Data pipelines must include row count checks, duplicate detection, null validation, and reconciliation against source system totals before production release.

Incident Escalation:
Critical incidents affecting customer reporting must be escalated to the engineering lead within 30 minutes. Root cause analysis must be documented within 48 hours.
