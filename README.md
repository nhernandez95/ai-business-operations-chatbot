# AI Business Operations Chatbot

## Business Problem
Companies have important knowledge buried inside SOPs, manuals, PDFs, and policy documents. Employees waste time searching or asking the same questions repeatedly.

## Solution
This chatbot retrieves relevant internal policy sections and generates an operational answer with source context.

## What It Demonstrates
- Retrieval-augmented generation concept
- Internal knowledge assistant design
- SOP automation
- Business operations workflow support
- Enterprise AI assistant positioning
